About Us

(Conference of Parties)

The COP Club at HPS aims to find innovative and effective solutions to combat climate change and promote sustainability through educational and interesting projects and activities. It is a platform to discuss and brainstorm ideas as well as work to implement them for the betterment of society and the environment. Implementation of solar panels in the school, rallies for raising awareness, experimenting with various types of technology to come up with use prototypes are just a few of our numerous activities.

The Hyderabad Public School, Begumpet is one of the oldest schools in India, established in 1923. HPS provides holistic education, facilitates students to achieve their optimum potential and equips them to meet the challenges of life. It believes in integrating academics with co-curricular activities and sports for the overall development of the student. Over the years, HPS has produced a multitude of successful alumni and has won numerous awards including the IGBC Green Schools Platinum 2018.

The larger picture

#Decarbonize

Centre for Global Education

The Centre for Global Education seeks to educate children and youth around the world using online technology to facilitate project-based, transformative learning, CGE breaks down traditional classroom barriers.

#Decarbonize Global Child Climate Program is a school-based program that connects primary and secondary children in over 70 countries to collaboratively learn, share, and act on climate issues.

Our Mission

Decarbonize. Decolonize!

At the COP Club, we aim to combat climate change and global warming through activities, projects and discussions which are educational as well as impactful. Our Vision is to empower the youth to take action against the climate crisis for a better tomorrow. The Climate Change Club is a forum for innovative thinking as well as expression and implementation of ideas.

Our Acheivements

and our plans for the future

  • Implemented a solar panel system to produce renewable electricity and conserve energy

  • Rallied in the streets of Hyderabad to raise awareness about climate change and to urge people to protect the environment

  • Created various scientific prototypes, such as the Thermo Drone and The Ocean Debris Cleaner to combat pollution

  • Compost Pit and Biogas plant in the works to convert food and organic waste into energy for efficient use
